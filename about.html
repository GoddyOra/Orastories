Author

Orastories was created by me, Goddy Ora. I’m an avid novel reader and a big fan of J.R.R. Tolkien’s Middle-earth legendarium and Robin James’s Cass Leary legal thriller series.

I started writing in my early teens with short fictional stories, and I completed my first full book at 19: Call Me Malcolm, a crime-romance thriller.

Over the years, I’ve also ghostwritten multiple books under different pen names, including “Mark Reed,” “Goddy Ora,” “Ali Judge,” and “Godwin Ora,” among others.

You can check out my Upwork profile for ghostwriting services.

I love writing contemporary romance and crime thrillers, and lately I’ve started exploring the fantasy fiction world in my newer work.

Orastories is an immersive reading platform designed to bring stories to life through cutting-edge technology and elegant design. Our mission is to create a sanctuary for readers and writers alike.

We believe that reading should be more than just consuming words on a screen. It should be an experience—a journey into worlds crafted by talented storytellers, enhanced by thoughtful design and intelligent features.

Our Vision

To democratize access to exceptional literature and create a community where readers can discover, engage with, and celebrate the stories that matter most to them.

Our Features

  • • Immersive Reader: A distraction-free reading environment designed for focus and engagement
  • • Smart Library: Organize and discover your favorite stories with intelligent categorization
  • • AI Assistant: Personalized recommendations powered by AI technology
  • • Dark/Light Modes: Reading experience optimized for your preferred lighting environment
  • • Minimalist Design: Beautiful, clean interface that gets out of the way of the story
